#  Workforce Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

The Workforce Attrition Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze employee turnover and workforce trends. The dashboard helps organizations understand attrition patterns, identify high-risk employee groups, and make informed HR decisions to improve employee retention.

## Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition rates across departments and job roles.
* Identify factors contributing to employee turnover.
* Monitor workforce demographics and employee distribution.
* Support data-driven HR and business decisions.

## Key Insights

* Department-wise attrition analysis
* Job role and employee performance insights
* Age group and gender distribution
* Salary and compensation analysis
* Employee tenure and experience trends
* Attrition comparison across different employee categories
